Prestige Finsbury Park

Prestige Finsbury Park is a large township destination of 25 acres situated adjacent to the aerotropolis within the IT/ Aerospace and Hardware Park being developed by the KIADB, which will be the hub in the future for the IT and Aerospace sectors.
